Spikeball Rules and Tips

  1. Setup
    • Assemble the Spikeball net.
    • Ensure the net is taut for consistent bounce.
    • Mark out a playing area if necessary.
  2. Basic Rules
    • Each team has up to three touches per possession.
    • The ball must bounce off the net to count as a valid hit.
    • Switch servering position to other openent every consecutive point.
  3. Advanced Tips
    • Spend time in the basement and get cracked at serving.
